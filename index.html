Step ONE

Find your httpd.conf.
Usually it can be found in /etc/httpd/conf/ directory.
Find a line that starts with "LogFormat", around line 500.

Step TWO

Choose elements that you want in your log, then drag and drop the to the space below.

    Copy the syntax. You can also see how you log will look like.
